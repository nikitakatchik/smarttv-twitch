# Install Twellie on a 2013 F-series Orsay TV from macOS

1. Download the installer for [⬇️ Apple Silicon](/releases/latest/download/twellie-orsay-host-macos-arm64.zip) or [⬇️ Intel](/releases/latest/download/twellie-orsay-host-macos-x64.zip).
2. Unzip the file on the Mac.
3. Right-click `Install-Twellie.command`, choose **Open**, then choose **Open** again.
4. Enter your Mac password when macOS asks. The installer needs port 80 for Orsay App Sync.
5. Leave the installer window open and note the IP address it prints.
6. On the TV, press **Menu**.
7. Go to **Smart Features** -> **Samsung Account** -> **Log In**.
8. Enter `develop` as the account name and confirm the login.
9. Open **Smart Hub**.
10. Open **More Apps**.
11. Open **Options** in the top-right corner.
12. Choose **IP Setting**.
13. Enter the IP address from step 5 and confirm.
14. Open **Options** again.
15. Choose **Start App Sync**.
16. Wait for the TV to show **Service installed**.
17. Open **Twellie** from Smart Hub.
18. Close the installer window on the Mac.
