# Install Twellie on Tizen with Apps2Samsung

Use this guide for supported Samsung Tizen TVs from 2015 and newer when you want
the native `.wgt` install path. Model, firmware, region, developer-mode access,
and Apps2Samsung behavior can vary, so device reports are welcome.

1. Download [⬇️ Twellie.wgt](/releases/latest/download/Twellie.wgt) to your computer.
2. Download [⬇️ Apps2Samsung](https://github.com/Apps2Samsung/Apps2Samsung/releases/latest) to your computer.
3. Connect the computer and the TV to the same network.
4. On the TV, press **Home** or **Smart Hub**.
5. Open **Apps**.
6. Enter **1 2 3 4 5** on the remote. If the remote has no number buttons, press **123** and use the on-screen keypad.
7. In the **Developer Mode** popup, set **Developer mode** to **On**.
8. Enter your computer's local IP address as **Host PC IP**.
9. Select **OK**.
10. Restart the TV. A full power-off and power-on is fine.
11. Start **Apps2Samsung** on the computer.
12. Sign in to your Samsung account in Apps2Samsung if it asks.
13. Select your TV in Apps2Samsung.
14. Choose **Custom WGT File**.
15. Select the `Twellie.wgt` file you downloaded in step 1.
16. Click **Install**.
17. Wait for Apps2Samsung to finish signing and installing the app.
18. On the TV, open **Apps** and launch **Twellie**.

For the TizenBrew path, use the [TizenBrew guide](tizenbrew.md).
